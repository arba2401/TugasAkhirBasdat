About

What actually is Adoption Center? Our purpose?

Adoption Center was first established in 2018 by Jedi Fathan as a place where people could saved homeless, abused, abandoned animals. We also treated animals that are found injured if needed to.

Saving lives. Unburdening shelters. Creating community to show our heart towards the rescued animals. Giving love and hugs for our rescued animals.

We strive to shatter the many negative stereotypes about abandoned animals. By encouraging people to adopt instead of buying a pet, and educating the public about the importance of spaying and neutering.

We can put an end to the unnecessary euthanasia of adoptable animals and realize a future where there are no more homeless pets.
